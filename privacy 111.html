PRIVACY POLICY

// EXILE STUDIO // ARCHIVE 001

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you register on our terminal, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used only for:

  • Sending you archive updates and new article notifications
  • Email marketing communications (with consent)
  • SMS marketing communications (with explicit opt-in)
  • Improving our service and user experience

3. SMS OPT-IN & CONSENT

By checking the SMS consent box, you explicitly authorize Exile Studio to send you text messages to the phone number you provide. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates apply. You can opt out anytime by texting STOP.

4. EMAIL MARKETING

You will receive emails from Exile Studio about archive updates, new articles, and related announcements. You can unsubscribe from these communications anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

5. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is encrypted and stored securely on Netlify and Klaviyo servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services to process your data:

  • Netlify (hosting & form collection)
  • Klaviyo (email & SMS marketing platform)
  • Zapier (data automation)

These services have their own privacy policies which you should review.

7. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use basic analytics and tracking to understand how visitors use our site. Klaviyo uses tracking pixels to monitor email engagement.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw SMS consent anytime

Contact us at terminal@exilestudioarchive.com to exercise these rights.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy anytime. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated timestamp below.

10. CONTACT

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at terminal@exilestudioarchive.com
